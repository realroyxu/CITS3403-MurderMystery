A murder mystery game is an engaging and interactive experience where players immerse themselves in a fictional story of intrigue and suspense. The primary objective is to solve a murder by carefully reading the provided story, analyzing clues, and ultimately identifying the killer. Players take on different roles such as detectives, suspects, or witnesses, each with unique backgrounds and motives. They must analyze clues, interrogate other characters, and piece together the mystery to solve the crime. The game typically concludes with a dramatic reveal of the murderer and the reasoning behind their actions.
